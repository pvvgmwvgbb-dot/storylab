# Transformations

This file stores the core cognitive transformations in the lesson.

## Transformation 1: Earth history to story need

Abstract:
Earth is billions of years old and changed over time.

Visible:
A timeline moves from space dust to hot Earth to oceans to dinosaurs.

Story function:
The learner is not just told history. They are moved into it.

## Transformation 2: Addition to staircase

Abstract:
`1 + 2 + 3 + ... + 10`

Visible:
Rows of square blocks forming a staircase.

Teaching point:
The sum is not a string of numbers. It is a shape.

## Transformation 3: Staircase to rectangle

Abstract:
The arithmetic series formula.

Visible:
Copy the staircase, rotate it, and join it to the original.

Discovery:
Two staircases form a rectangle.

For 1 to 10:

```text
2 * sum = 10 * 11
sum = 10 * 11 / 2 = 55
```

## Transformation 4: Specific pattern to general rule

Specific:
`1 + 2 + ... + 10`

General:
`(first + last) * count / 2`

Plain-language version:
The total is the average value times the number of values.

## Transformation 5: Changed movement to changed sequence

Story change:
The learner can now jump two blocks higher each time.

Math change:
The rows become 2, 4, 6, 8, 10.

Teaching point:
An arithmetic series does not have to start at 1 or grow by 1. The important idea is equal change.
