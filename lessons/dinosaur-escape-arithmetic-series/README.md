# Dinosaur Escape: Arithmetic Series

A visual story lesson that connects Earth history, dinosaurs, and arithmetic series.

The learner is transported to the dinosaur age. A dinosaur starts chasing them. To escape, they must build a staircase to reach a high ledge.

The math appears because the story needs it:

- one-step climbing creates `1 + 2 + 3 + ... + 10`
- two-step climbing creates `2 + 4 + 6 + 8 + 10`
- the staircase can be copied and rotated to reveal a rectangle
- the formula appears after the pattern is seen

This lesson is designed for image-first teaching. Text should be minimal on screen. The teacher tells the story aloud.

## Files

- `story.yaml`: structured lesson source
- `narration.md`: teacher narration script
- `scenes.md`: visual scene plan
- `questions.md`: teacher questions
- `transformations.md`: core cognitive transformations
